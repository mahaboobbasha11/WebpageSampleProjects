Dr Reddy Foundation Founder

Dr. Reddy’s Foundation was founded by Dr. Kallam Anji Reddy, a renowned scientist, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories, established in 1984, aimed to make affordable medicines and become a hub for pharmacy research. About a decade later, Dr. Anji Reddy started the Dr. Reddy’s Foundation in 1996. The foundation’s vision is to assist young disadvantaged individuals in accessing quality education and developing skills to meet modern challenges12. Dr. Anji Reddy received the Padma Shri in 2001 and later the Padma Bhushan for his contributions to the Indian pharmaceutical industry The current Chairman of Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories and Dr. Reddy’s Foundation is K. Satish Reddy, who actively supports vocational education and sustainable livelihoods for Indian youth. Another key figure is Anuradha Gunupati, the Founder Trustee, who guides education and livelihood-generation programs and is also involved in promoting Indian arts and culture1. The foundation’s journey has impacted over 2 million lives since its inception3.
Certainly! Dr. Reddy’s Foundation focuses on several key initiatives to empower disadvantaged individuals in India. Some of these include: Skill Development Programs: The foundation offers vocational training and skill development courses to enhance employability. These programs cover various sectors such as healthcare, retail, and hospitality.
Livelihood Enhancement: Dr. Reddy’s Foundation supports livelihood-generation activities by promoting entrepreneurship, self-help groups, and community-based enterprises. This helps individuals create sustainable income sources.
Education Initiatives: The foundation works to improve access to quality education. It provides scholarships, conducts remedial classes, and supports school infrastructure development.
Healthcare Awareness: Dr. Reddy’s Foundation raises awareness about health and hygiene through community health camps, workshops, and outreach programs.
Women Empowerment: The foundation focuses on empowering women by providing skill training, promoting financial literacy, and encouraging women-led enterprises.
