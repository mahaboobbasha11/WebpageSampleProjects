Dr. Reddy’s Foundation (DRF) was established in 1996 with a mission to educate and skill young people, enabling them to fast-track their way toward better livelihood options. The foundation’s early programs focused on children’s education and skill development among young individuals from lower-income households1. Driven by the vision of Dr. K. Anji Reddy, a renowned scientist, entrepreneur, and philanthropist, DRF aimed to empower individuals by providing them with affordable quality education. The foundation established a school and a vocational college to achieve this goal. Over the years, DRF’s interventions have impacted the lives of over 2 million people
